Egg-ceptional Scrambled Eggs

Watch a video on how to scramble an egg with Professor Yolk

Make this awesome egg scramble when plain scrambled eggs and salt just doesn't cut it anymore. You can add any ingredient that you wish, but for here I have included the three main ingredients that you need to make this "egg-ceptional!"

  • PrepTime: 15M
  • Total Time: 20M
  • Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup meat of choice (optional)
  • 6 grape tomatoes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Beat eggs in bowl with cheese and if you choose, some meat such as ham.
  2. Heat non-stick pan with butter on high until butter begins to melt, then turn on medium heat.
  3. Once butter is melted, pour in eggs.
  4. Use your heat-resistant spatula to begin mixing the eggs and ingredients in the pan so it continues to cook the eggs at an even rate. Medium/low heat will prevent the eggs from expanding too fast and will allow you to control the consistency.
  5. Keep stirring until eggs are beginning to firm, but are still wet.
  6. Then pour in tomatoes and continue to cook eggs.
  7. Cook the eggs until slightly soft/moist but not wet.
  8. Turn your heat off and grab your plate. Serve the eggs and enjoy with toast, orange juice, and maybe some bacon.

Nutrition

  • Serving Size: One Person/94g
  • Calories: 319 cal
  • Fat Content: 20.2 g
